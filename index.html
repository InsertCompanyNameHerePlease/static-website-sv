Full-angle Camera Sensor Software

Synclair Vision specializes in the development and maintenance of camera systems designed specifically for medium to large-sized drones. Our cutting-edge camera system serves as an auxiliary component to the primary camera system onboard the drone. With the help of machine learning, our camera system effectively aids the main camera system in determining points of interest.
