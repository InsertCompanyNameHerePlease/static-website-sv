About Us

Synclair Vision is an innovative software company, originated from a collaborative venture of 7 university students with diverse academic backgrounds. Our team comprises five M.Sc in Comptuter Science students and/or graduates from Linköping University, a M.Sc in Industrial Engineering and Management student, and a M.Sc student in Applied Physics and Electrical Engineering.

Together, we've dedicated several years to pioneering the future of aerial drone camera systems. Our diverse educational backgrounds have uniquely positioned us to approach challenges creatively, allowing us to develop and share our vision of cutting-edge aerial drone technology with the world. At Synclair Vision, we're not just engineering the future; we're reimagining it through the multifaceted lens of technology, business, and innovation.

Our Vision

To revolutionize drone technology by providing users with 360-degree visibility through our innovative camera system, eliminating blind spots and enhancing situational awareness.

Our Mission

To empower our customers with unparalleled visibility and efficiency during drone operations, offering flexible business models and cutting-edge AI technology to meet their evolving needs while striving for excellence in the drone industry.

Our Goal

To become the industry standard for drone cameras, making our lightweight and compact system a ubiquitous feature on drones worldwide.
